STEM Bootcamps

We offer dynamic in-person and online bootcamps designed specifically for Afghan girls to explore the exciting world of STEM. These workshops focus on programming, web development, and robotics. Through hands-on projects and teamwork, participants build real-world skills, develop problem-solving abilities, and gain the confidence to pursue careers in science and technology.

Mentorship

Our mentorship program connects young Afghan girls with experienced women professionals in tech fields. These mentors provide not only technical guidance but also emotional support, career advice, and role modeling. Through regular one-on-one sessions and group meetups, mentees develop stronger self-esteem and clearer pathways to success in their academic and professional lives.

Scholarships

To ensure equal opportunity, we provide need-based scholarships, internet packages, and access to digital learning materials for underprivileged girls across Afghanistan. Our goal is to eliminate financial and technical barriers so that every girl, regardless of her background, can learn, grow, and become a leader in the digital world. the Scholarships offer more for those who really appeal this opportunity and have a very high background.
